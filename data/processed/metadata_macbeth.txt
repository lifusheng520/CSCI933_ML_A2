PLAY TITLE: Macbeth

DESCRIPTION:
A dark tragedy exploring the physical and psychological effects of political ambition on those who seek power for its own sake. Set in Scotland, it follows the transformation of a noble soldier into a bloodthirsty tyrant.

MAIN STORY THREAD:
- Three witches prophesy that Macbeth will become King of Scotland. 
- Encouraged by his wife, Macbeth murders King Duncan and seizes the throne.
- Consumed by guilt and paranoia, Macbeth commits a series of murders to secure his power.
- Lady Macbeth descends into madness and death.
- Macduff and Malcolm lead an army against Macbeth, fulfilling the witches' final, cryptic prophecies.
- Macbeth is defeated and killed, and the rightful heir, Malcolm, is crowned.

ROLE MATRIX:
- Macbeth: A Scottish general and Thane of Glamis; driven by ambition and prophecy to become a tyrant.
- Lady Macbeth: Macbeth's wife; deeply ambitious and the initial driving force behind the murder of Duncan.
- King Duncan: The benevolent King of Scotland whom Macbeth murders.
- Banquo: Macbeth's brave and loyal friend; his descendants are prophesied to inherit the throne.
- Macduff: A Scottish nobleman who becomes the leader of the resistance against Macbeth.
- The Three Witches: Supernatural beings who provide the prophecies that trigger Macbeth's actions.
- Malcolm: King Duncan's eldest son; the rightful heir to the throne.
