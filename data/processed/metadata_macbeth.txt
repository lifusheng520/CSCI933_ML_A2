PLAY TITLE: Macbeth

DESCRIPTION:
A dark tragedy exploring the physical and psychological effects of political ambition on those who seek power for its own sake. Set in Scotland, it follows the transformation of a noble soldier into a bloodthirsty tyrant. [cite: 3]

MAIN STORY THREAD:
- Three witches prophesy that Macbeth will become King of Scotland. [cite: 3]
- Encouraged by his wife, Macbeth murders King Duncan and seizes the throne. [cite: 3]
- Consumed by guilt and paranoia, Macbeth commits a series of murders to secure his power. [cite: 3]
- Lady Macbeth descends into madness and death. [cite: 3]
- Macduff and Malcolm lead an army against Macbeth, fulfilling the witches' final, cryptic prophecies. [cite: 3]
- Macbeth is defeated and killed, and the rightful heir, Malcolm, is crowned. [cite: 3]

ROLE MATRIX:
- Macbeth: A Scottish general and Thane of Glamis; driven by ambition and prophecy to become a tyrant. [cite: 3]
- Lady Macbeth: Macbeth's wife; deeply ambitious and the initial driving force behind the murder of Duncan. [cite: 3]
- King Duncan: The benevolent King of Scotland whom Macbeth murders. [cite: 3]
- Banquo: Macbeth's brave and loyal friend; his descendants are prophesied to inherit the throne. [cite: 3]
- Macduff: A Scottish nobleman who becomes the leader of the resistance against Macbeth. [cite: 3]
- The Three Witches: Supernatural beings who provide the prophecies that trigger Macbeth's actions. [cite: 3]
- Malcolm: King Duncan's eldest son; the rightful heir to the throne. [cite: 3]
