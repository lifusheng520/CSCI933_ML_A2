PLAY TITLE: Hamlet

DESCRIPTION:
A tragedy set in Denmark following Prince Hamlet's quest for revenge against his uncle, Claudius, who murdered Hamlet's father and seized the throne.

MAIN STORY THREAD:
- The Ghost of King Hamlet reveals the murder.
- Hamlet feigns madness to investigate.[cite: 2]
- Hamlet stages a play to confirm Claudius's guilt.[cite: 2]
- Hamlet accidentally kills Polonius.[cite: 2]
- A rigged duel leads to the deaths of the royal family.[cite: 2]

ROLE MATRIX:
- Hamlet: Prince of Denmark; son of Gertrude.[cite: 2]
- Claudius: King; antagonist; brother of late King.[cite: 2]
- Gertrude: Queen; mother of Hamlet.[cite: 2]
- Ophelia: Polonius’s daughter; Hamlet’s romantic interest.[cite: 2]
- Horatio: Hamlet’s loyal confidant and survivor.[cite: 2]
